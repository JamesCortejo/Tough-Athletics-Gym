Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Data Deletion

At Tough Athletics Gym, we respect your right to privacy and data control. You can request deletion of your personal data through the following methods:

Option 1: Through the App

  1. Log in to your Tough Athletics Gym account
  2. Go to "Settings" in your profile
  3. Click on "Privacy & Data"
  4. Select "Request Data Deletion"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions

Option 2: Via Email

Send an email to our privacy team with the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Email: privacy@toughathleticsgym.com

Option 3: In Person

Visit Tough Athletics Gym and speak with our management team to submit a data deletion request in person.

What Happens After Request

  • Confirmation: We will send a confirmation email within 24 hours
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity
  • Processing: Data deletion will be completed within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You will receive a final confirmation once completed

Information That Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Profile data and pictures
  • Membership history
  • Check-in records
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal data stored in our systems

Information We May Retain

For legal and regulatory purposes, we may retain certain information for a limited time:

  • Financial transaction records (as required by law)
  • Security and fraud prevention data
  • Anonymous, aggregated data that cannot identify you
